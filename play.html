At Play we started to create a lot of blog posts and as well as posting them to Medium we wanted a section on our website to show them all. I had some down time after a project so our creative director gave me the task of designing the blog home and an example post. A lot of the styling was already set as it had to fit with our current website. I knew we were not going to be posting a lot so I decided to make the layout alternate one per row then two per row rather than a consistent three or two per row. Images coming soon.
