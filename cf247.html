Carfinance

App

Client
Carfinance247
Role
Junior Designer
When
August, 2016

Carfinance247 approached us with the challenge to create a seamless journey to allow customers to complete the whole car finance process online. We started off with a service design phase to study the existing process and market. We wanted to find current pain points in the process and also see what currently works well. We decided next to run three week long design sprints exploring key areas of the service. Ideas generated were made into prototypes and put into user testing at the end of each week. Finally using our findings from the earlier weeks we moved into the production design phase and created the final designs to hand over to the Carfinance247 dev team.
