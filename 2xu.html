Starting my first job in the industry straight after college I felt I may have lacked skills in some fundamental areas of visual design. Layout and type being two specific areas so I decided to try and learn more about these topics and then put it into practice. I started by reading a few different books then picking a project to work on. I decided to design a blog as it is content heavy and layout and type are two very important pieces to the designs. I tried to create a design that is visually appealing and at the same time would engage the audience. Overall I am happy with the final designs but looking back at the project what I value more is what I learned throughout the process. I feel much more confident now when making type and layout decisions.
